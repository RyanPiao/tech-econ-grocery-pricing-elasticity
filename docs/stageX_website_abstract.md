On a 60,000-session synthetic panel, higher delivered prices are consistently associated with lower conversion (descriptive FE elasticity around -0.05), with similar direction across user and market segments. Data engineering quality checks passed with zero missing fee-version assignments and zero timestamp-immutability violations.
